Brazilian Pastel

Ingredients

Goya flour empanadas

warm water

pinch of salt

1lb ground beef

1 red onion

3 cloves garlic

250g of any cheese you like

vegetable or sunflower oil

Directions

Place flour empanada on cutting board. Dip your finger into warm water and gently tap on all sides of the dough. Chop the onion and sauté in oil until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until brown. Chop the garlic and add to the beef. Cook until beef is light brown. Salt to taste. Fill the dough with cheese or beef and seal the flour dough with a fork, pressing the top and bottom together to close. Heat the oil in a pan. Add the pastels and fry. Turn half way through when golden brown. Serve.

Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

3 tbsp sour cream

1lb lean beef

1 tsp salt

2 tbsp butter

1 tbsp olive oil

1 tbsp all-purpose flour

1 cup beef stock

1 medium onion

Directions

Cut beef into strips. Sprinkle meat with salt and set aside. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a medium saucepan. Blend in flour and cook for 2 minutes. Gradually stir in beef broth, stirring until thickened and smooth. Heat 1tbsp of butter and 1tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; add onions and cook. Add the meat strips and quickly brown on all sides. Add the beef and onions to the beef sauce. Cover and cook on low for 10 minutes; stir in sour cream and heat through. Serve.

Beef Quesadillas

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef

1⁄2 tsp dried oregano

1⁄2 tsp salt

4 flour tortillas

2 tbsp butter melted

1 1⁄3 cups shredded taco cheese

1 tsp paprika

Directions

In a skillet, cook the beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Stir in the oregano and salt. Cook over medium-low heat for 3-4 minutes or until heated through. Brush one side of each tortilla with butter. Spoon 1/2 cup of the meat mixture over half of the unbuttered side. Sprinkle with 1/3 cup cheese; fold in half. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 475 degrees for 10 minutes or until crisp and golden browned. Cut into wedges. Serve.

Lasagna

Ingredients

Lasagna noodles

3/4 pound ground beef

1 medium onion, diced

3 garlic cloves, minced

2 cans of any tomato sauce

3/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp ground pepper

1 carton ricotta cheese

4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

Boil water to cook noodles, drain. In a skillet, saute beef and onion until meat is slight brown. Stir in tomato sauce, garlic, salt and pepper. Preheat oven to 375°. Spread meat sauce into an ungreased baking dish. Layer with noodles and a third of the ricotta mixture. Add 1 cup mozzarella cheese and 2tbsp Parmesan cheese. Repeat twice. Top with remaining meat sauce and cheeses. Bake for 25 minutes. Serve.

Cous Cous W/ Ground Beef

Ingredients

2 cups uncooked cousocus

3 cups broths defatted

1 cup carrots cut into small cubes

2 tsp butter

1 cup ground beef

1 cup onion chopped

4 tsp butter

salt & pepper to taste

Directions

In a saucepan, heat the beef broth and carrots over medium heat cook until carrots are soft. Melt the butter in the broth and add the couscous. Turn down the heat, cover and let stand for 5 minutes. In a large skillet, heat the butter until the butter is melted. Add the onions to the skillet for 3 to 5 minutes. Once the onions are tender, add the ground beef. Add a pinch of salt and cook until the meat is ready, and has a medium brown color, for approximately 20 to 25 minutes. Serve.

Cherry Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

1lb pasta

salt

4 medium cloves garlic, sliced

extra-virgin olive oil

1 1/2lbs cherry tomatoes

1 cup chopped fresh basil

ground black pepper

Parmesan cheese

Directions

Place pasta in a large pot and cover with water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Boil until al dente. Heat garlic and 4 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat until garlic is softened about 3 minutes. Add tomatoes and cook until tomatoes begin to burst. Stir in basil and season to taste with salt and pepper. When pasta is cooked, drain. Add pasta to sauce and increase heat to medium-high. Remove from heat and grate Parmesan cheese. Serve.
